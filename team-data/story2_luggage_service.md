# Story 2: Luggage Service

## Concept
- When a passenger checks in luggage at the airport, the luggage is delivered directly to their hotel at the destination.
- The only exception is the customs area, where the passenger must handle the luggage personally.

## Workflow
1. Check-in at airport: Luggage is tagged and registered.
2. Luggage is tracked and transported using a secure logistics network.
3. At customs, the passenger retrieves and clears their luggage.
4. After customs, the luggage is handed back to the service and delivered to the hotel.
